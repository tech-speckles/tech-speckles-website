Setup & Installation

  1. Open any Google Sheet → Extensions → Add-ons → Get add-ons and search for Smart Column Cleaner, or open the Marketplace listing directly.
  2. Click Install and grant requested permissions.
  3. In your Sheet: Extensions → Smart Column Cleaner → Open Cleaner.
  4. (Optional) Click (change) next to Duplicate mode to set how duplicates are handled.
Permissions: The add-on needs access to the active spreadsheet to read/write selected cells and store lightweight settings in document properties. It also reads your Google account email to check license status.
