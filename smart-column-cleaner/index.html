Smart Column Cleaner

Clean messy sheets in seconds: De‑duplicate, Trim whitespace, Change case, and remove Non‑ASCII/emoji. Everything runs inside Google—no external servers.

Install from Google Workspace Marketplace  ·  View Help & FAQ

Features

  • De-Duplicate Cells (by column) — keep first; blank or highlight others.
  • Remove duplicate rows (by column key) — move duplicate rows to a separate sheet for review.
  • Clear Duplicates within Row — remove duplicates across selected columns.
  • Trim whitespace — removes leading/trailing spaces.
  • Change case — lowercase / UPPERCASE / Proper Case.
  • Remove Non‑ASCII — strip emojis and odd symbols.

How it works

  1. Open a Google Sheet → Extensions → Smart Column Cleaner → Open Cleaner.
  2. (Optional) Click (change) to choose your duplicate mode.
  3. Select a range/column and run a tool.

Privacy & pricing

Free plan: clean up to 300 cells/day. Pro plan removes limits. The add‑on processes data only within your Google account and stores lightweight settings in document properties. See Privacy and Terms.
