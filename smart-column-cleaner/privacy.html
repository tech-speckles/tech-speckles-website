Overview

Smart Column Cleaner is a Google Sheets add-on by Tech Speckles. It processes only the spreadsheet data you select to perform actions such as trimming whitespace, changing case, removing non-ASCII characters, and de-duplicating cells.

Data we access and process

  • Sheet data: values in your selected range to execute the chosen clean-up action.
  • User email: read from your Google account to check license status via Google’s Marketplace licensing (“LicensingApp”).
  • Document properties: we store lightweight settings in the current spreadsheet (e.g., duplicate-mode) and a daily usage counter to enforce the free quota.

What we don’t do

  • No external servers: data isn’t sent to or stored on Tech Speckles servers.
  • No advertising or sale of data.
  • No access to files outside the active spreadsheet.

Retention

Settings and counters are stored in the spreadsheet’s Document Properties. Daily counters reset each day; you may delete properties by copying content to a new spreadsheet or via Google Apps Script.

Security

All processing occurs within Google’s infrastructure under your account permissions. Licensing is verified using Google Workspace Marketplace systems.

Your choices

  • Uninstall the add-on at any time from Google Workspace Marketplace.
  • Contact us to request information or assistance.
