Advantages

Data is processed entirely within Google’s infrastructure. No external servers are used. Settings are stored as lightweight document properties.

Each tool does one job really well. Clear copy, sensible defaults, minimal clicks.

Fast support

Responses are typically provided within one business day (IST). A sheet snippet or step list helps ensure quick reproduction of issues.
